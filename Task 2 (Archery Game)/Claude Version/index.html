How to Play

Click anywhere to shoot an arrow at the moving target!

Hit the target to score points based on where you hit:

  • 🎯 Gold Center: 50 points
  • 🔴 Red Ring: 30 points
  • 🔵 Blue Ring: 15 points
  • ⚫ Black Ring: 10 points
  • ⚪ White Ring: 5 points

You have 60 seconds and 3 arrows!

The target gets faster and smaller as you score!
